About Us

Built On Trust, Driven By Excellence

DSG Electrical provides dependable, high-quality electrical services for homes, businesses, and urgent callouts. Our team is committed to delivering clean workmanship, reliable service, and tailored solutions that precisely meet each client's needs.

From smaller domestic installations to large-scale commercial projects, we pride ourselves on professionalism, meticulous attention to detail, and a genuine customer-first approach that puts your safety and satisfaction above everything else.

What We Do

Our Electrical Services

Whether you need routine maintenance, a full rewire, or an urgent emergency callout, DSG Electrical has the expertise and equipment to get the job done right.

🏠

Residential Electrician

Expert electrical services tailored for homes — from full rewires and consumer unit upgrades to socket installations, lighting design, and EV charger fitting. We keep your home safe, compliant, and efficient.

🏢

Commercial Electrician

Professional electrical solutions for offices, retail units, warehouses, and commercial spaces of every scale. We minimise disruption, meet all regulatory standards, and deliver robust systems built to last.

⚡

24/7 Emergency Electrician

Fast-response emergency support when you need it most. Our team is on call around the clock to handle power failures, tripped circuits, exposed wiring, and any other urgent electrical hazard.

🔌

EV Charging Installation

Fully certified home and workplace EV charger installations. We assess your existing supply, recommend the right charger for your vehicle, and carry out a clean, SEAI-grant-eligible installation.

💡

Lighting & Smart Systems

Transform your space with bespoke lighting schemes, smart home automation, and energy-efficient LED retrofits. We design systems that are as practical as they are impressive.

🛡️

Safety Testing & Certification

Comprehensive electrical condition reports (EICR), periodic inspections, and certification for landlords, buyers, and businesses. Ensure your property meets current safety regulations.
